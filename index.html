Working towards a better future

The students at AlphaLocke are dedicated to the cause for a better source of energy through nuclear reactors. Through their vast amount of research and knowledge, students at AlphaLocke are helping spread awareness about the utility of nuclear reactors as well as shining light on the possible issues that come with them.
